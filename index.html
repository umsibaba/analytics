Added new Adaptive Algo layer for Trend Exhaustion factoring
16 hours ago

The Adaptive Algo layer is like having three different smoke detectors in your house that each work in different ways, but you only need one of them to go off to know there's a problem. Here's what it does:

What Problem Does It Solve?

When prices move too far, too fast from their normal levels (or moving averages move too far apart from each other), the market often becomes "exhausted" and is likely to reverse or pause. But detecting this reliably is tricky.

How the Adaptive Method Works:

The Adaptive method combines three different approaches to detect when things are getting extreme:

  • "Z-Score Method": Measures how unusual the current situation is compared to normal.
  • Bollinger-based method: Tracks when prices move outside standard deviation bands.
  • Momentum fade detection: Identifies when price movement velocity slows despite continued directional movement.
